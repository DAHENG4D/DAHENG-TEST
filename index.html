How to Play Daheng 4D

1. Choose Your Numbers

Select any 4-digit number from 0000 to 9999. You can also choose 'Lucky Pick' for a randomly generated number.

2. Select Your Bet Type

Choose "Big" (permissible, ordinary, or sum) or "Small" (permissible only) according to your preference.

3. Place Your Bet

Decide on your bet amount for each chosen number and bet type. Minimum bet is RM 1.
